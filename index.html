Aigerim Keutayeva

I am a Graduate Research Assistant at Nazarbayev University in Astana, Kazakhstan.

I received my B.S. degree in Robotics and Mechatronics and M.S. degree in Robotics from Nazarbayev University, Astana, Kazakhstan, in 2021 and 2023, respectively. Since 2019, I have been a Research Assistant with the School of Engineering and Digital Sciences, Nazarbayev University, and a member of Young Researchers Alliance , Astana. My research interests include machine learning, brain–computer interfaces, object recognition, and digital twin. My Master's journey was advised by Berdakh Abibullaev and funded by the NU Research Grant Program.

Integrating Machine Learning Model and Digital Twin System for Additive Manufacturing
Nursultan Jyeniskhan, Aigerim Keutayeva, Gani Kazbek, Md.Hazrat Ali, Essam Shehab
IEEE Access, 2023
full text / video

This paper proposes a digital twin system framework for additive manufacturing that integrates machine learning models, employing Unity, OctoPrint, and Raspberry Pi for real-time control and monitoring.

Robust subject-independent BCIs using Attention Mechanism based Deep Learning models
Aigerim Keutayeva
School of Engineering and Digital Sciences, 2023

This thesis investigates robust subject-independent BCIs using attention mechanismbased deep learning models on 4 ERP- and 4 MI-based BCI datasets. Access by request only.
